Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

1. Introduction

FinPal ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal finance companion app that helps you track income, expenses, and spending patterns. This Privacy Policy explains what information FinPal collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

By using FinPal, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2. Information We Collect

FinPal is designed to store most of your data locally on your device. We may collect or process the following types of information:

  • Profile information — such as your display name and avatar selection.
  • Financial records — income and expense entries you create, including amounts, dates, categories, payment methods, and optional notes.
  • Custom settings — categories, payment methods, and other preferences you configure in the app.
  • AI chat messages — messages you send to the in-app AI assistant, stored locally on your device and sent to our AI provider when you request a response.
  • App usage data — basic technical information such as app version and device type, only if required for app functionality or crash diagnostics.

FinPal does not require you to create an account, and we do not collect passwords, bank login credentials, or direct access to your bank accounts.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide core app features such as expense tracking, analytics, and transaction history.
  • Personalize your profile and app experience.
  • Generate AI-powered financial guidance when you use the assistant feature.
  • Validate spending against your income and show relevant alerts.
  • Improve app reliability, performance, and user experience.

4. Local Storage

Most FinPal data is stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage (including Hive and SharedPreferences). This means your financial records remain on your phone unless you choose to share them elsewhere.

You can delete your data at any time by clearing app data from your device settings or uninstalling the app. Uninstalling FinPal will remove locally stored information from your device.

5. Third-Party Services

FinPal uses third-party services to power certain features. When you use the AI assistant, your chat messages may be transmitted to Google Gemini (Google LLC) to generate responses. Google's handling of that data is governed by its own privacy policies.

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with third parties when necessary to operate a feature you actively use, or when required by law.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information stored within the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your device secure, including using a screen lock and keeping your operating system up to date.

7. Children's Privacy

FinPal is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, update, or delete information stored in the app.
  • Withdraw consent for optional features such as the AI assistant.
  • Request information about how your data is processed.

Because most data is stored locally, you can manage or delete it directly within the app or through your device settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of FinPal after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
