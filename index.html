ℹ️ This Privacy Policy applies to the Seven mobile application, developed by Shubham Patel. By using this Application, you agree to the practices described below. The Application is provided as a free service on an "AS IS" basis.
01

Information We Collect

The Application is designed with a minimal data footprint. We collect only what is technically necessary to provide the service.

We may collect:

  • Basic technical request/response data required to load content from TMDB (The Movie Database)
  • Optional profile data you choose to enter: name, gender, date of birth, and avatar selection

We explicitly do NOT collect:

  • IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Precise or approximate location data (GPS or otherwise)
  • Device-specific identifiers or hardware details
  • Personal data via AI or automated profiling technologies
  • Any data from users who do not create a profile — no login is required
02

How We Use Information

Any information collected or voluntarily provided is used solely to:

  • Operate and provide Application features
  • Save your profile and preferences locally on your device
  • Fetch and display movie/TV metadata and related media from TMDB
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the Application
  • Send important service-related notices when necessary
🔒 We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties. Your data is used exclusively to provide the features described above.
03

Data Storage

All profile and preference data is stored exclusively and locally on your device. No personal data is transmitted to or stored on any server owned or operated by the Service Provider.

You can delete all stored data at any time by uninstalling the Application through the standard uninstall process on your device or via the Google Play Store.

💡 For deletion assistance, contact us at seven.dev987@gmail.com and we will respond promptly.

The Application uses TMDB (The Movie Database) to fetch movie and TV show content. TMDB may receive limited technical request data (such as query parameters) to serve content. The Service Provider does not control TMDB's data practices.

Your Rights Under GDPR (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) grants you the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access

Request information about what data we hold about you.

Rectification

Request correction of any inaccurate data.

Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data.

Restriction

Request that we limit how your data is used.

Portability

Request a copy of your data in a portable format.

Objection

Object to processing of your personal data.

ℹ️ Since all profile data is stored locally on your device and not on any server, most of these rights are exercised directly by you through the Application or by uninstalling it. The legal basis for processing optional profile data is your explicit consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR). You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting the Application.
